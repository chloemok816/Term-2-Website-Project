About

This website is about one of the world most popular game "Mario Kart 8 Deluxe", this game is released on April 27.2017. You can play this on Nintendo platforms. This game is a great game and a lot of people enjoy playing it. This is one of the game in the Mario Kart series. It's a single, multiplayer game, online play or wireless play and there are different modes you can play such as battle mode; vs race and grand prix and time trials.
